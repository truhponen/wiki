Zigbee coordinator is physical device that works in tandem with [Zigbee router](/zigbee-router) and [Zigbee2MQTT](/zigbee2mqtt).

Current Zigbee adapter is SONOFF Zigbee 3.0 USB Dongle Plus ZBDongle-P.
