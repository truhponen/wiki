## Zigbee buttons

Zigbee buttons are used to control [lights](/lights) and [audio devices](/audio-devices).

* Ikea buttons integrated through [Zigbee2MQTT](/zigbee2mqtt)
* Philips Hue buttons are integrated through [Hue bridge](/hue-bridge)
